Project Structure

Governance

GUIDE-AI is governed by a lean, two-tier structure designed to enable fast decision-making while maintaining scientific integrity and IHI JU compliance.

Decision-Making Bodies

General Assembly All consortium partners. Meets annually. Highest decision-making authority on strategic matters, budget reallocation above threshold, and consortium composition changes.
Steering Committee One representative per partner. Meets quarterly. Responsible for day-to-day project governance, milestone tracking, and cross-WP coordination.
Project Coordinator Charité – Universitätsmedizin Berlin (Germany). Leads the Steering Committee, serves as primary contact with IHI JU, and oversees legal and financial management through WP01.
Scientific Advisory Board Independent external experts. Reviews scientific quality, provides strategic input, and assesses alignment with clinical needs and regulatory requirements.

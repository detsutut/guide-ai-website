Our Mission

Bridging the gap between guidelines and bedside care

Clinical practice guidelines summarise the best available evidence — yet applying them to individual patients with multiple chronic conditions remains a complex, time-consuming challenge. GUIDE-AI makes guideline-based decision support fast, reliable, and explainable.

🧠

Large Language Models

We fine-tune and align state-of-the-art LLMs on curated clinical guideline corpora to enable natural-language guideline query and reasoning.

🏥

Clinical Integration

Guideline Navigators are validated in real clinical workflows across multiple EU health systems, ensuring safety, usability, and clinical relevance.

🔍

Explainable & Safe

Every recommendation is traceable to its source guideline. We rigorously assess AI safety, hallucination risk, and regulatory compliance.

The Challenge & Our Response

From complex guidelines to clinical action

⚠ The Clinical Challenge

  • Clinical guidelines run to hundreds of pages and are updated frequently, making it impossible for busy clinicians to stay current
  • Patients with multiple comorbidities fall between guideline silos — no single document addresses their full complexity
  • Existing clinical decision support tools are rigid, keyword-based, and unable to handle natural clinical questions
  • Guideline adherence in heart failure, CKD, COPD, and asthma remains suboptimal, leading to avoidable hospitalisations
  • Limited access to guideline expertise in primary care and underserved settings widens health inequalities

✦ The GUIDE-AI Approach

  • LLM-based Guideline Navigators that understand free-text clinical questions and retrieve evidence-grounded answers
  • Cross-guideline reasoning: handling patients with comorbid conditions across heart failure, CKD, COPD, asthma, and IBD
  • Transparent source attribution — every answer links back to the specific guideline recommendation
  • Prospective clinical validation in diverse EU healthcare settings with measurable adherence outcomes
  • Open science approach: validated datasets and model benchmarks shared with the research community
Project Structure

Work Packages

GUIDE-AI is organised into seven work packages covering the full lifecycle from clinical requirements to real-world validation and dissemination.

WP01

Project Management & Coordination

Overall project governance, risk management, financial reporting, and quality assurance across all consortium partners.

WP02

Clinical Requirements & Knowledge Engineering

Defining clinical use cases, curating guideline corpora, and creating the ontological framework for cross-disease reasoning.

WP03

Data Governance & Infrastructure

GDPR-compliant data infrastructure, federated learning architecture, and secure processing pipelines across sites.

WP04

AI & LLM Development

Fine-tuning, alignment, and benchmarking of LLMs on clinical guideline content, with hallucination detection and safety guardrails.

WP05

Clinical Validation & Real-World Testing

Prospective evaluation of Guideline Navigators in clinical settings across multiple EU health systems.

WP07

Dissemination & Communication

Scientific dissemination, stakeholder engagement, patient communication, and policy outreach activities.

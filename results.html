Project Updates

News

10 Apr 2025
Project Update

GUIDE-AI Consortium Convenes for Kick-Off Meeting

Representatives from all partner institutions gathered to officially launch GUIDE-AI, align on project objectives, establish working group structures, and plan the first year of activities.

The kick-off marked the formal start of collaborative work across the seven work packages. Partners exchanged introductions, confirmed roles, and agreed on communication and reporting channels.

01 Feb 2025
Announcement

GUIDE-AI Receives Funding from the Innovative Health Initiative

The IHI Joint Undertaking has awarded GUIDE-AI funding under IHI Call 9 (Grant Agreement No. 101253015), recognising the project's potential to transform AI-assisted clinical decision support across European health systems.

The IHI JU is a public-private partnership supporting large-scale collaborative research to accelerate development of health innovations in Europe.

15 Jan 2025
Overview

Introducing GUIDE-AI: Intelligent Guideline Navigators for Chronic Disease Management

An introduction to the GUIDE-AI project — its scientific vision, the clinical problem it addresses, the role of large language models in guideline navigation, and how the European consortium will tackle this challenge over four years.

GUIDE-AI targets five chronic disease areas: heart failure, chronic kidney disease, COPD, asthma, and IBD as an exploratory focus.

AI-powered guideline navigators for chronic disease management. An IHI JU-funded European research project (Grant No. 101253015).
